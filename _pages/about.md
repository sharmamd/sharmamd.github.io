Hi, I'm Madhu, a geodynamic modeller with a passion for understanding the subduction process. As a PhD researcher at GEO3BCN-CSIC, Barcelona, Spain I investigate how crustal-scale heterogeneities within subducting oceanic plates influence the evolution of subduction zones. Using numerical geodynamic models and high-performance computing, I explore how geological features such as seamounts, oceanic plateaus, aseismic ridges, and sedimentary basins affect slab geometry, trench migration, and lithospheric deformation. My research is carried out primarily using ASPECT, an open-source finite-element geodynamic modelling code that uses adaptive mesh refinement to efficiently resolve complex thermo-mechanical processes across a wide range of spatial scales.
