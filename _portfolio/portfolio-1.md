---
title: "Subduction Dynamics driven by Crustal Heterogeneities in the Subducting Plate"
excerpt: |
  Subducting oceanic plates are not uniform; they contain inherited structures such as seamounts, oceanic plateaus, and sediment-rich basins. Using 2D geodynamic models, we demonstrate that strong heterogeneities can slow or even halt subduction, whereas weak heterogeneities accelerate trench retreat and, remarkably, can trigger slab breakoff in the absence of continental collision.
